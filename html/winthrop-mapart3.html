TLP Transportation (TLP --- Traveling Little Passengers) is a conveyance service providing safe, flexible, reliable and affordable door-to-door transportation for children belonging to various age groups. Besides, we also offer transportation to and from local hospitals for appointments. We are currently carrying out our operations at Medford, Chelsea, Revere, East Boston, Saugus, Everett, Somerville, Winthrop and Malden in MA.
Without an iota of doubt today’s ultrafast-paced life has put our choices at stake, pushing them to their minimal limits so that reaching school on time, participating in extracurricular activities or having to leave work at inconvenient times have all turned into a difficult prospect to achieve on regular basis. The parents have got to confront and bear the brunt most as they are required to be present at their kids’ school for their pick-and-drop as well as their professional workplace, simultaneously!
TLP Transportation happens to be a unique shuttle service offering smart conveyance solutions in the form of pre-arranged or pre-scheduled pick-and-drop from school, daycare or anywhere your kid needs to be. Our services are student-centric, personalized and highly dependable which helps families maintain work-life balance. We cater to utmost safety and security of your kids by distancing them from the perils of walking to and from school alone and treading along busy streets.
