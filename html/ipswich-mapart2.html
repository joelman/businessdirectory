Fika and Friends provide animal assisted therapy in Boston, MA. AAT is an emerging form of restorative intervention in which animals are utilized as tools for customers to acquire self-understanding and psychological growth. Pet and equine assisted psychiatric therapy is a type of treatment, a field of mental health that recognizes the bond in between animals and people and the potential for emotional healing that can happen when a relationship is formed between the 2 types.
Fika and Pals offer inspirational, leisure and educational interactions that boost individuals's quality of life. Literacy programs assist kids improve their reading abilities by reading. Further, the animal interaction inspires rehabilitation clients to walk again, teaches kids in special-education classrooms essential life abilities, and facilitates therapy sessions in psychological health centers.
Animal helped therapy is believed to be an effective short-term healing approach for both individuals and households, attending to a variety of mental health problems, including behavioral problems, depression and stress and anxiety, low self-confidence, consuming conditions, ADD/ADHD, post distressing stress disorder, and relationship issues. While there is a need for research study to support anecdotal evidence of the efficiency of animal assisted psychotherapy.
